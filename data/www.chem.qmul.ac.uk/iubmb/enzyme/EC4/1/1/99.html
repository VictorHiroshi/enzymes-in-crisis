IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 4.1.1.99

Accepted name: phosphomevalonate decarboxylase

Reaction: ATP + (R)-5-phosphomevalonate = ADP + phosphate + isopentenyl phosphate + CO2

For diagram of reaction click here.

Systematic name: ATP:phosphomevalonate carboxy-lyase (adding ATP; isopentenyl-phosphate-forming)

Comments: The enzyme is part of the mevalonate pathway in archaea.
