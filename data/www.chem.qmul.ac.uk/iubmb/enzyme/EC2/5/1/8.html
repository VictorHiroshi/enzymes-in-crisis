IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.5.1.8

Transferred entry: tRNA isopentenyltransferase. As it is now known that the substrate is dimethyallyl diphosphate, the enzyme has been transferred to EC 2.5.1.75, tRNA dimethylallyltransferase.

[EC 2.5.1.8 created 1972, deleted 2009]
