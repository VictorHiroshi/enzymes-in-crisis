EC 2.7.5

Phosphotransferases with regeneration of donors, apparently catalysing intramolecular transfers

EC 2.7.5 In the early editions of Enzyme Nomenclature, phosphomutases were listed here under the heading 'Phosphotransferases with regeneration of donors, apparently catalysing intramolecular transfers'. They are now listed in sub-subgroup EC 5.4.2 on the basis of the overall isomerization reaction.
