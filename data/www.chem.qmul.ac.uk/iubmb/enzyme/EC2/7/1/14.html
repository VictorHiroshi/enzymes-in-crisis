IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.14

Accepted name: sedoheptulokinase

Reaction: ATP + sedoheptulose = ADP + sedoheptulose 7-phosphate

Other name(s): heptulokinase; sedoheptulokinase (phosphorylating)

Systematic name: ATP:sedoheptulose 7-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9030-63-1

References:

1. Ebata, M., Sato, R. and Bak, T. The enzymic phosphorylation of sedoheptulose. J. Biochem. (Tokyo) 42 (1955) 715-725.

[EC 2.7.1.14 created 1961]
