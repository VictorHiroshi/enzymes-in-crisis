IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.23

Accepted name: NAD+ kinase

Reaction: ATP + NAD+ = ADP + NADP+

For diagram of reaction click here.

Other name(s): DPN kinase; nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide kinase (phosphorylating); nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide kinase; NADK

Systematic name: ATP:NAD+ 2'-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, GTD, KEGG, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 9032-66-0
