IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.7

Accepted name: mannokinase

Reaction: ATP + D-mannose = ADP + D-mannose 6-phosphate

Other name(s): mannokinase (phosphorylating); D-fructose (D-mannose) kinase

Systematic name: ATP:D-mannose 6-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9030-54-0

References:

1. Bueding, E. and MacKinnon, J.A. Hexokinases of Schistosoma mansoni. J. Biol. Chem. 215 (1955) 495-506.

[EC 2.7.1.7 created 1961]
