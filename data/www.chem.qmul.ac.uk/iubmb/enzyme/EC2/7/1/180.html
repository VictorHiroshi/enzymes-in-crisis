IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.180

Accepted name: FAD:protein FMN transferase

Reaction: FAD + [protein]-L-threonine = [protein]-FMN-L-threonine + AMP

Glossary: FMN = flavin mononucleotide = riboflavin-5′-phosphate

Other name(s): flavin transferase; apbE (gene name)

Systematic name: FAD:protein riboflavin-5′-phosphate transferase

Comments: The enzyme catalyses the transfer of the FMN portion of FAD and its covalent binding to the hydroxyl group of an L-threonine residue in a target flavin-binding protein such as the B and C subunits of EC 1.6.5.8, NADH:ubiquinone reductase (Na+-transporting). Requires Mg2+.
