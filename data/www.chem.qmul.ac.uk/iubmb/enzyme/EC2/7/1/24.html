IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.24

Accepted name: dephospho-CoA kinase

Reaction: ATP + 3'-dephospho-CoA = ADP + CoA

For diagram of reaction click here.

Other name(s): dephosphocoenzyme A kinase (phosphorylating); 3'-dephospho-CoA kinase; dephosphocoenzyme A kinase

Systematic name: ATP:dephospho-CoA 3'-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, GTD, KEGG, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 9026-83-9
