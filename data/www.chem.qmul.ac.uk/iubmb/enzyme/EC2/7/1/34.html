IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.34

Accepted name: pantetheine kinase

Reaction: ATP + pantetheine = ADP + pantetheine 4'-phosphate

Other name(s): pantetheine kinase (phosphorylating)

Systematic name: ATP:pantetheine 4'-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9026-49-7

References:

1. Novelli, G.D. Enzymatic synthesis and structure of CoA. Fed. Proc. 12 (1953) 675-682.

[EC 2.7.1.34 created 1961]
