IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.7.1.13

Accepted name: dehydrogluconokinase

Reaction: ATP + 2-dehydro-D-gluconate = ADP + 6-phospho-2-dehydro-D-gluconate

Other name(s): ketogluconokinase; 2-ketogluconate kinase; ketogluconokinase (phosphorylating); 2-ketogluconokinase

Systematic name: ATP:2-dehydro-D-gluconate 6-phosphotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9030-56-2

References:

1. Frampton, E.W. and Wood, W.A. Purification and properties of 2-ketogluconokinase from Aerobacter aerogenes. J. Biol. Chem. 236 (1961) 2578-2580.

[EC 2.7.1.13 created 1961]
