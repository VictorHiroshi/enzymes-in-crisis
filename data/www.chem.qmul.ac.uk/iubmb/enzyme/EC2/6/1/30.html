IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.6.1.30

Accepted name: pyridoxamine—pyruvate transaminase

Reaction: pyridoxamine + pyruvate = pyridoxal + L-alanine

Other name(s): pyridoxamine-pyruvic transaminase

Systematic name: pyridoxamine:pyruvate aminotransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, GTD, KEGG, Metacyc, UM-BBD, CAS registry number: 9023-38-5

References:

1. Wada, H. and Snell, E.E. Enzymatic transamination of pyridoxamine. II. Crystalline pyridoxamine-pyruvate transaminase. J. Biol. Chem. 237 (1962) 133-137.

[EC 2.6.1.30 created 1972]
