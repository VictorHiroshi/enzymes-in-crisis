IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.4.1.190

Accepted name: luteolin-7-O-glucuronide 2"-O-glucuronosyltransferase

Reaction: UDP-glucuronate + luteolin 7-O-β-D-glucuronide = UDP + luteolin 7-O-[β-D-glucuronosyl-(1→2)-β-D-glucuronide]

For diagram click here.

Other name(s): uridine diphosphoglucuronate-luteolin 7-O-glucuronide glucuronosyltransferase; LMT; UDP-glucuronate:luteolin 7-O-glucuronide-glucuronosyltransferase

Systematic name: UDP-glucuronate:luteolin-7-O-β-D-glucuronide 2"-O-glucuronosyltransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 115490-51-2

References:

1. Schulz, M. and Weissenböck, G. Three specific UDP-glucuronate-flavone-glucuronosyl-transferases from primary leaves of Secale cereale. Phytochemistry 27 (1988) 1261-1267.

2. Anhalt, S. and Weissenböck, G. Subcellular localization of luteolin glucuronides and related enzymes in rye mesophyll. Planta 187 (1992) 83-88.

[EC 2.4.1.190 created 1992]
