IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.4.1.28

Accepted name: glucosyl-DNA β-glucosyltransferase

Reaction: Transfers a β-D-glucosyl residue from UDP-glucose to a glucosylhydroxymethylcytosine residue in DNA

Other name(s): T6-glucosyl-HMC-β-glucosyl transferase; T6-β-glucosyl transferase; uridine diphosphoglucose-glucosyldeoxyribonucleate β-glucosyltransferase

Systematic name: UDP-glucose:D-glucosyl-DNA β-D-glucosyltransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9030-15-3

References:

1. Kornberg, S.R., Zimmerman, S.B. and Kornberg, A. Glucosylation of deoxyribonucleic acid by enzymes from bacteriophage-infected Escherichia coli. J. Biol. Chem. 236 (1961) 1487-1493.

[EC 2.4.1.28 created 1965]
