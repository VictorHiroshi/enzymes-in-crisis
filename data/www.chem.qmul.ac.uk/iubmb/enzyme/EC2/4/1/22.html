IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.4.1.22

Accepted name: lactose synthase

Reaction: UDP-α-D-galactose + D-glucose = UDP + lactose

Other name(s): UDP-α-D-galactose—glucose galactosyltransferase; N-acetyllactosamine synthase; uridine diphosphogalactose-glucose galactosyltransferase; lactose synthetase; UDP-galactose:D-glucose 4-β-D-galactotransferase; UDP-galactose:D-glucose 4-β-D-galactosyltransferase

Systematic name: UDP-α-D-galactose:D-glucose 4-β-D-galactosyltransferase

Comments: The enzyme is a complex of two proteins, A and B. In the absence of the B protein (α-lactalbumin), the enzyme catalyses the transfer of galactose from UDP-galactose to N-acetylglucosamine (EC 2.4.1.90 N-acetyllactosamine synthase).

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 9030-11-9
