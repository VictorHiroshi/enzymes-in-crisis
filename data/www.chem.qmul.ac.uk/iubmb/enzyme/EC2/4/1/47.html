IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 2.4.1.47

Accepted name: N-acylsphingosine galactosyltransferase

Reaction: UDP-α-D-galactose + an N-acylsphingosine = UDP + a D-galactosylceramide

Other name(s): UDP galactose-N-acylsphingosine galactosyltransferase; uridine diphosphogalactose-acylsphingosine galactosyltransferase; UDP-galactose:N-acylsphingosine D-galactosyltransferase

Systematic name: UDP-α-D-galactose:N-acylsphingosine D-galactosyltransferase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 37277-56-8

References:

1. Fujino, Y. and Nakano, M. Enzymic synthesis of cerebroside from ceramide and uridine diphosphate galactose. Biochem. J. 113 (1969) 573-575. [PMID: 5807218]

[EC 2.4.1.47 created 1972]
