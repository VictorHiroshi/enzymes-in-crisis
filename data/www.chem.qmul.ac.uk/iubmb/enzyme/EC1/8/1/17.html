IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.8.1.17

Accepted name: dimethylsulfone reductase

Reaction: dimethyl sulfoxide + H2O + NAD+ = dimethyl sulfone + NADH + H+

For diagram of reaction click here.

Comments: A molybdoprotein.
