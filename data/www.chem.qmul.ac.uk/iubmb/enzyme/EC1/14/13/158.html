IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.14.13.158

Accepted name: amorpha-4,11-diene 12-monooxygenase

Reaction: amorpha-4,11-diene + 3 O2 + 3 NADPH + 3 H+ = artemisinate + 3 NADP+ + 4 H2O (overall reaction)
(1a) amorpha-4,11-diene + O2 + NADPH + H+ = artemisinic alcohol + NADP+ + H2O
(1b) artemisinic alcohol + O2 + NADPH + H+ = artemisinic aldehyde + NADP+ + 2 H2O
(1c) artemisinic aldehyde + O2 + NADPH + H+ = artemisinate + NADP+ + H2O

For diagram of reaction click here.

Other name(s): CYP71AV1

Systematic name: amorpha-4,11-diene,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (12-hydroxylating)

Comments: A heme-thiolate protein (P-450). Cloned from the plant Artemisia annua (sweet wormwood). Part of the biosynthetic pathway of artemisinin.
