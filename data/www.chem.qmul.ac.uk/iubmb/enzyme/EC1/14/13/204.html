IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.14.13.204

Accepted name: long-chain acyl-CoA ω-monooxygenase

Reaction: (1) oleoyl-CoA + NADPH + H+ + O2 = 18-hydroxyoleoyl-CoA + NADP+ + H2O
(2) linoleoyl-CoA + NADPH + H+ + O2 = 18-hydroxylinoleoyl-CoA + NADP+ + H2O

Other name(s): long-chain acyl-CoA ω-hydroxylase; CYP86A22 (gene name); CYP52M1 (gene name)

Systematic name: long-chain acyl-CoA,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (ω-hydroxylating)

Comments: The enzymes from solanaceous plants are involved in the biosynthesis of stigmatic estolide, a lipid-based polyester that forms a major component of the exudate.
