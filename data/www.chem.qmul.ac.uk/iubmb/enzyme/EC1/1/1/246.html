IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.1.1.246

Transferred entry: pterocarpin synthase. This activity is now known to be catalysed by two enzymes, EC 1.1.1.348, vestitone reductase and EC 4.2.1.139, medicarpin synthase.

[EC 1.1.1.246 created 1992, modified 2012, deleted 2013]
