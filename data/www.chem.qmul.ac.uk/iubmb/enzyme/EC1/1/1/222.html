IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.1.1.222

Accepted name: (R)-4-hydroxyphenyllactate dehydrogenase

Reaction: (R)-3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)lactate + NAD(P)+ = 3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)pyruvate + NAD(P)H + H+

Other name(s): (R)-aromatic lactate dehydrogenase; D-hydrogenase, D-aryllactate

Systematic name: (R)-3-(4-hydroxyphenyl)lactate:NAD(P)+ 2-oxidoreductase

Comments: Also acts, more slowly, on (R)-3-phenyllactate, (R)-3-(indole-3-yl)lactate and (R)-lactate.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 101754-02-3

References:

1. Bode, R., Lippoldt, A. and Birnbaum, D. Purification and properties of D-aromatic lactate dehydrogenase an enzyme involved in the catabolism of the aromatic amino acids of Candida maltosa. Biochem. Physiol. Pflanzen 181 (1986) 189-198.

[EC 1.1.1.222 created 1989]
