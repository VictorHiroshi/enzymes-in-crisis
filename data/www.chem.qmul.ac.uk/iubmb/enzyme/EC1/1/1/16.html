IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.1.1.16

Accepted name: galactitol 2-dehydrogenase

Reaction: galactitol + NAD+ = D-tagatose + NADH + H+

Other name(s): dulcitol dehydrogenase

Systematic name: galactitol:NAD+ 2-oxidoreductase

Comments: Also converts other alditols containing an L-threo-configuration adjacent to a primary alcohol group into the corresponding sugars.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 9028-23-3

References:

1. Shaw, D.R.D. Polyol dehydrogenases 3. Galactitol dehydrogenase and D-iditol dehydrogenase. Biochem. J. 64 (1956) 394-405.

[EC 1.1.1.16 created 1961]
