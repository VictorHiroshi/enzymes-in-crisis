IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.1.1.251

Accepted name: galactitol-1-phosphate 5-dehydrogenase

Reaction: galactitol-1-phosphate + NAD+ = L-tagatose 6-phosphate + NADH + H+

Systematic name: galactitol-1-phosphate:NAD+ oxidoreductase

Comments: Contains zinc.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 60120-43-6

References:

1. Nobelmann, B. and Lengeler, J.W. Sequence of the gat operon for galactitol utilization from a wild-type strain EC3132 of Escherichia coli. Biochim. Biophys. Acta 1262 (1995) 69-72. [PMID: 7772602]

[EC 1.1.1.251 created 1999]
