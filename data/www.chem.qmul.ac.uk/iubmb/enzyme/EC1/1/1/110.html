IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 1.1.1.110

Accepted name: indolelactate dehydrogenase

Reaction: (indol-3-yl)lactate + NAD+ = (indol-3-yl)pyruvate + NADH + H+

Other name(s): indolelactate:NAD+ oxidoreductase

Systematic name: (indol-3-yl)lactate:NAD+ oxidoreductase

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, GTD, KEGG, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 37250-41-2

References:

1. Jean, M. and DeMoss, R.D. Indolelactate dehydrogenase from Clostridium sporogenes. Can. J. Microbiol. 14 (1968) 429-435. [PMID: 4384683]

[EC 1.1.1.110 created 1972]
