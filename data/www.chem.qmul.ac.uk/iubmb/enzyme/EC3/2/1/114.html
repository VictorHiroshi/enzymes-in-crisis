IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.2.1.114

Accepted name: mannosyl-oligosaccharide 1,3-1,6-α-mannosidase

Reaction: Hydrolysis of the terminal 1,3- and 1,6-linked α-D-mannose residues in the mannosyl-oligosaccharide Man5(GlcNAc)3

For diagram click here.

Other name(s): mannosidase II; exo-1,3-1,6-α-mannosidase; α-D-mannosidase II; α-mannosidase II; α1-3,6-mannosidase; GlcNAc transferase I-dependent α1,3[α1,6]mannosidase; Golgi α-mannosidase II; ManII; 1,3(1,6)-α-D-mannosidase

Systematic name: 1,3-(1,6-)mannosyl-oligosaccharide α-D-mannohydrolase

Comments: Involved in the synthesis of glycoproteins.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 82047-77-6
