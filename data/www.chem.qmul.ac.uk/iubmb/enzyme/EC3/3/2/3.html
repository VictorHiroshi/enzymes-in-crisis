IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.3.2.3

Transferred entry: epoxide hydrolase. Now known to comprise two enzymes, microsomal epoxide hydrolase (EC 3.3.2.9) and soluble epoxide hydrolase (EC 3.3.2.10).
