IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.25

Accepted name: sulfate-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + sulfateout = ADP + phosphate + sulfatein

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (sulfate-importing)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. A bacterial enzyme that imports sulfate and thiosulfate anions.
