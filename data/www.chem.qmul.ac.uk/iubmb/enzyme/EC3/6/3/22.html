IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.22

Accepted name: nonpolar-amino-acid-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + nonpolar amino acidout = ADP + phosphate + nonpolar amino acidin

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (nonpolar-amino-acid-transporting)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. Comprises bacterial enzymes that import Leu, Ile and Val.
