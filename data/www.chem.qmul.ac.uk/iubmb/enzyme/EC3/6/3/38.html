IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.38

Accepted name: capsular-polysaccharide-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + capsular polysaccharidein = ADP + phosphate + capsular polysaccharideout

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (capsular-polysaccharide-exporting)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. An enzyme that exports capsular polysaccharide from Gram-negative bacteria.
