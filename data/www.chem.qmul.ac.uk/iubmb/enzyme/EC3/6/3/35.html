IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.35

Accepted name: manganese-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + Mn2+out = ADP + phosphate + Mn2+in

Other name(s): ABC-type manganese permease complex

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (manganese-importing)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. A bacterial enzyme that imports Mn2+, Zn2+ and iron chelates.
