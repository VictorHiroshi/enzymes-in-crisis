IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.36

Accepted name: taurine-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + taurineout = ADP + phosphate + taurinein

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (taurine-importing)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. A bacterial enzyme that imports taurine.
