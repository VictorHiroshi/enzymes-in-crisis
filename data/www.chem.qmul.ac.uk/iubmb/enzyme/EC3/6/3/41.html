IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.41

Accepted name: heme-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + hemein = ADP + phosphate + hemeout

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (heme-exporting)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. An enzyme found in Gram-negative bacteria that exports heme.
