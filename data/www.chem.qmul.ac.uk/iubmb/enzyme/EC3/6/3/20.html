IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.20

Accepted name: glycerol-3-phosphate-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + glycerol-3-phosphateout = ADP + phosphate + glycerol-3-phosphatein

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (glycerol-3-phosphate-importing)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. A bacterial enzyme that imports phosphorylated glycerol.
