IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.6.3.18

Accepted name: oligosaccharide-transporting ATPase

Reaction: ATP + H2O + oligosaccharideout = ADP + phosphate + oligosaccharidein

Systematic name: ATP phosphohydrolase (disaccharide-importing)

Comments: ABC-type (ATP-binding cassette-type) ATPase, characterised by the presence of two similar ATP-binding domains. Does not undergo phosphorylation during the transport process. A bacterial enzyme that imports lactose, melibiose and raffinose.
