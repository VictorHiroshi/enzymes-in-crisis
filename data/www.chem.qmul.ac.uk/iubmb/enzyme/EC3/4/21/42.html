IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.21.42

Accepted name: complement subcomponent C

Reaction: Cleavage of ArgAla bond in complement component C4 to form C4a and C4b, and Lys(or Arg)Lys bond in complement component C2 to form C2a and C2b: the "classical" pathway C3 convertase

Other names: C1 esterase; activated complement C1s; complement C

Comments: Activated from proenzyme C1s in plasma by complement subcomponent C . In peptidase family S1 (trypsin family)

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 80295-70-1
