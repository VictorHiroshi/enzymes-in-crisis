IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.76

Accepted name: flavastacin

Reaction: Hydrolyses polypeptides on the amino-side of Asp in -XaaAsp-. Acts very slowly on -XaaGlu

Comments: A zinc metalloendopeptidase in peptidase family M12 (astacin family), secreted by the bacterium Flavobacterium meningosepticum . The specificity is similar to that of (EC 3.4.24.33), peptidyl-Asp metalloendopeptidase from Pseudomonas fragi but the two are reported to be structurally dissimilar

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 167973-66-2
