IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.66

Accepted name: choriolysin L

Reaction: Hydrolysis of the inner layer of fish egg envelope. Also hydrolysis of casein and small molecule substrates such as succinyl-Leu-Leu-Val-Tyr7-(4-methyl)coumarylamide

Other names: teleost hatching enzyme (component); low choriolytic enzyme (LCE)

Comments: Known from the teleost fish Oryzias latipes (medaka). Efficient dissolution of the egg membrane requires concerted action with choriolysin H. A 24 kDa peptidase family M12 (astacin family)

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 177529-15-6
