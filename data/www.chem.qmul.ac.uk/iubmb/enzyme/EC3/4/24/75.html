IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.75

Accepted name: lysostaphin

Reaction: Hydrolysis of the -GlyGly- bond in the pentaglycine inter-peptide link joining staphylococcal cell wall peptidoglycans

Other names: glycyl-glycine endopeptidase

Comments: A zinc-dependent, 25-kDa endopeptidase from Staphylococcus simulans. Lyses cells of S. aureus, in particular, by its action on the cross-bridges of the cell wall. Type example of peptidase family M23

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 9011-93-2
