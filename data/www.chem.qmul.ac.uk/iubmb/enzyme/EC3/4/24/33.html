IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.33

Accepted name: peptidyl-Asp metalloendopeptidase

Reaction: Cleavage of XaaAsp, XaaGlu and Xaacysteic acid bonds

Other names: endoproteinase Asp-N; peptidyl-Asp metalloproteinase

Comments: A metalloenzyme isolated from Pseudomonas fragi. Useful in protein sequencing applications because of its limited specificity. In peptidase family M72.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 55576-49-3
