IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.18

Accepted name: meprin A

Reaction: Hydrolysis of protein and peptide substrates preferentially on carboxyl side of hydrophobic residues

Other names: endopeptidase-2; meprin-a; meprin; N-benzoyl-L-tyrosyl-p-aminobenzoic acid hydrolase; PABA-peptide hydrolase; PPH

Comments: A membrane-bound metalloendopeptidase of rat and mouse kidney and intestinal brush borders, and salivary ducts. Differences from neprilysin (EC 3.4.24.11) include insensitivity to phosphoramidon and thiorphan. PABA-peptide hydrolase is a very similar enzyme found in human intestinal microvilli [4]. In peptidase family M12 (astacin family). Formerly included in EC 3.4.24.11

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 148938-24-3
