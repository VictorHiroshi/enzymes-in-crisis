IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.24.20

Accepted name: peptidyl-Lys metalloendopeptidase

Reaction: Preferential cleavage in proteins: -XaaLys- (in which Xaa may be Pro)

Other names: Armillaria mellea neutral proteinase; peptidyllysine metalloproteinase

Comments: From the honey fungus Armillaria mellea. In peptidase family M35 (deuterolysin family). Formerly EC 3.4.99.32.

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, PDB, CAS registry number: 65979-41-1
