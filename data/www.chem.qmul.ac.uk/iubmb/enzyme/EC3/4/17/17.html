IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.4.17.17

Accepted name: tubulinyl-Tyr carboxypeptidase

Reaction: Cleavage of the -GluTyr bond to release the C-terminal tyrosine residue from the native tyrosinated tubulin. Inactive on Z-Glu-Tyr

Other names: carboxypeptidase-tubulin; soluble carboxypeptidase; tubulin-tyrosine carboxypeptidase; tubulin carboxypeptidase; tubulinyltyrosine carboxypeptidase; tyrosinotubulin carboxypeptidase; tyrosyltubulin carboxypeptidase; TTCPase; brain I carboxypeptidase

Comments: Active at neutral pH, from brain

Links to other databases: BRENDA, EXPASY, KEGG, MEROPS, Metacyc, CAS registry number: 73050-23-4
