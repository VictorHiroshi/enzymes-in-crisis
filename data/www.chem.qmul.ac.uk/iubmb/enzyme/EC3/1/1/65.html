EC 3.1.1.65

Accepted name: L-rhamnono-1,4-lactonase

Reaction: L-rhamnono-1,4-lactone + H2O = L-rhamnonate

Other name(s): L-rhamno-γ-lactonase; L-rhamnono-γ-lactonase; L-rhamnonate dehydratase
