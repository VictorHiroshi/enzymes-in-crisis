EC 3.1.1.16

Deleted entry: 4-carboxymethyl-4-hydroxyisocrotonolactonase. This reaction was due to a mixture of EC 5.3.3.4 (muconolactone D-isomerase) and EC 3.1.1.24 (3-oxoadipate enol-lactonase)

[EC 3.1.1.16 created 1961, deleted 1972]
