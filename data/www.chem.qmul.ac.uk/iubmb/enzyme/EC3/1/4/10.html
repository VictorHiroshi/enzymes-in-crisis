IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 3.1.4.10

Transferred entry: now EC 4.6.1.13, phosphatidylinositol diacylglycerol-lyase. As there is no hydrolysis of the inositol 1,2-cyclic phosphate formed, previous classification of the enzyme as a hydrolase was incorrect.

[EC 3.1.4.10 created 1972, modified 1976, deleted 2002]
