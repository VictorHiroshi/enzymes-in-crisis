IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 5.1.3.39

Accepted name: L-erythrulose 4-phosphate epimerase

Reaction: L-erythrulose 4-phosphate = D-erythrulose 4-phosphate

Other name(s): lerI (gene name)

Systematic name: L-erythrulose-4-phosphate 3-epimerase

Comments: The enzyme, characterized from the bacterium Mycobacterium smegmatis, participates in the degradation of L-threitol.
