IUBMB Enzyme Nomenclature

EC 5.5.1.21

Accepted name: copal-8-ol diphosphate synthase

This enzyme was discovered at the public-review stage to have been misclassified and so was withdrawn. See EC 4.2.1.133, copal-8-ol diphosphate hydratase.

(EC 5.5.1.21 created 2012, deleted 2012)
